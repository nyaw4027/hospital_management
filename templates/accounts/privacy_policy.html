Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information to provide better medical services to all our patients. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email, and phone number when you register.
  • Medical Records: Diagnoses, prescriptions, and lab results managed by our doctors.
  • Activity Logs: System actions (logins, role changes) for security auditing.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used to:

  • Facilitate healthcare delivery between doctors and patients.
  • Generate billing and financial reports for the hospital management.
  • Maintain security and verify staff credentials through our Audit Trail.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not share your medical information with third parties unless required by law. All data is encrypted and accessible only to authorized personnel based on their System Role (Doctor, Manager, Lab, etc.).

4. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to request a copy of your medical history or ask for account deactivation through the hospital administration.

Questions about this policy? Contact our administration at privacy@hospital.com.
